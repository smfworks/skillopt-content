# Public content checklist (generic)

Use before shipping any public longform (blog, essay, docs, research note).

## Required pass

1. **Draft** for the correct surface and audience.
2. **Triage** — list improvement hypotheses; select **2–3 max**. Prefer:
   - argumentative rigor (claims supported)
   - insight novelty (non-obvious contribution)
   - over cosmetic polish
3. **Plan concrete edits** — exact section, change type, ready-to-insert prose (no placeholders).
4. **Apply** only those edits.
5. **Safety / privacy gate** (define your org policy locally):
   - [ ] No private personal data
   - [ ] No unapproved secrets or credentials
   - [ ] Citations / sources honest where claims require them
6. **Ship**
   - [ ] Metadata / frontmatter / images correct for your stack
   - [ ] Build or channel check passes
   - [ ] Live or staging verify when applicable

## One-line rule

**Before anything public ships: 2–3 rigor/novelty edits max, concrete prose, safety/privacy gate, then publish.**

## Not included here

Organization-specific voice, family/privacy policies, social CTAs, and brand handles belong in a **private profile** under `profiles/` (not forced as defaults in this kit).
